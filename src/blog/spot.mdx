Spot arrived on a Tuesday and immediately apologized — or at least, that's what it looked like when the gait controller re-planned around a dropped hard hat.

Over the next month the crew stopped flinching when it walked past. By month two, someone had given it a name tag. By month three, it was running inspection rounds nobody wanted to do in the rain.
