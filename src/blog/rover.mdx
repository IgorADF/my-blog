Sol 482. The wind kicked up overnight and coated the solar panels in a fine red dust. Power is down, but not out.

The team back home says we have another crater to visit before the season turns — layered rock, maybe a hint of ancient water. The rover doesn't know it's alone out here, and that, in some way, is a mercy.
